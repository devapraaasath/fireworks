Firecrackers Safety Tips

1. Keep a Safe Distance

Always maintain a safe distance from lit firecrackers to avoid injuries.

2. Light One at a Time

Only light one firecracker at a time and step back immediately.

3. Use Outdoors Only

Firecrackers should only be used outdoors, in an open space away from buildings.

4. Supervise Children

Keep children under close supervision and teach them firecracker safety rules.
